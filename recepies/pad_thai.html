Pad Thai

Pad Thai and why I love it

I have first discovered Pad Thai when on a trip to Ljubljana with my girlfriend Anja. The Mexican resteurant where we wanted to eat was busy and a Thai place was right around the corner. At first I didn't know what to order but I like nuts so I decided to go with Pad Thai that had chicken and peanuts. From the first bite I loved it, the crunch of the peanuts, the softness of the noodles combined with an amazing tasting souce that tastes nothing like I am used to.

Making Pad Thai

Making Pad Thai is very quick and simple. Once you have all the igredients ready and chopped up it takes about 5 minutes to make. This is an easy "healthy" mid week meal. You can make it even faster by preparing some stuff in advance.

Ingredients

  • Pad Thai Sauce
  • Rice Noodles
  • Unsalted peanuts
  • Green onions
  • Mug bean sprouts
  • Garlic
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Put the noodles in cold water for an hour
  2. Put the eggs in a seperate bowl, slat them and scrammble them. Let them rest for 10min.
  3. Slice up green onions thinly, add to a bowl
  4. Chop up the peanuts, add with green onions
  5. Thinly slice garlic and add it with other igredients. (Don't make the slices too small so that they don't burn)
  6. Cut up the chicken
  7. Into a hot pan add oil and start frying the chicken. Let it get about two thirds done.
  8. Add the mixture of green onions, peanuts and gardic that you prepared earlier
  9. Fry and stir for about 30s or just enough for the onions to get slightly soft
  10. Push everything on the sides and pour the eggs into the empty space on the bottom.
  11. Throw the noodles over the egg so it gets nicely incoperated
  12. Stir until the egg hardens
  13. Add Mug bean sprouts
  14. Add Pad Thai souce
